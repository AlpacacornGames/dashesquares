Dashes & Squares is a 2D platformer survival game where you are in a procedural generated not-infinite scene. Survive infinite amounts of rounds with squares (and not squares) that want to kill you! Get coins, upgrade and survive.
Features:
-Round based survival game.
-Survival mode (Normal and hardcore difficulties).
-Race mode.
-Procedural not-infinite generated scene for survival mode.
-Squares!
-Dashes!
-Skins.
-Not square shapes!
-GameJolt Integration.
-I said squares?
Upcoming features:
-Modding!
-Multi language support.
-More squares!
